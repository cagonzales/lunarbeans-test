Our Story

Lunar Beans is an elite coffee experience, It comes from coffee beans grown 238,900 mi above sea level in volcanic, lunar soil. Lunar Beans is 100% sourced from coffee beans digested and excreted by wild, cage-free moon cats that roam freely.

The Process

We gather beans by carefully combing the lunar surface for wild moon cat dropping. The Coffee beans are washed and the brought to our Venus facility where they are roasted to perfection.
